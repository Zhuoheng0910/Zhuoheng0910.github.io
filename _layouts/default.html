Zhuoheng Wang

zhuoheng22 [at] mails.tsinghua.edu.cn

Hi! I am an undergraduate student at Tsinghua University, majoring in Mechanics & Interdisciplinary Engineering.

Currently, I am a Research Assistant at the Laboratory for Intelligent Decision and Autonomous Robots (LIDAR) at Georgia Tech, advised by Prof. Ye Zhao and Prof. Anqi Wu. Previously, I was a Research Assistant at Tsinghua Robot Control Lab (RCL) in Department of Automation, advised by Prof. Mingguo Zhao. I was also fortunate to be a member of DISCOVER Lab at Institute of AI Research in Tsinghua University, advised by Prof. Guyue Zhou.

My research interests include Legged Robotics, Robot Learning and Control, and Athletic Intelligence. I am particularly interested in Humanoid Robot.
