Hi! I am a final-year undergrad in Mechanics & Interdisciplinary Engineering at Tsinghua University. Currently, I am working on Humanoid Control with Shengbo Eben Li and Hang Zhao.

Previously, I was a Research Assistant at LIDAR Group at Georgia Tech, supervised by Ye Zhao and Anqi Wu. I was also fortunate to work with Mingguo Zhao, Guyue Zhou, and Weibin Gu at Tsinghua University.
