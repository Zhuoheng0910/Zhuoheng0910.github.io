Xuxin Cheng (程旭欣)

I am currently a visiting student at UC Berkeley advised by Prof. Koushil Sreenath. I am working on robot learning of bipedal legged robot Cassie in HRL.

I received my B.S. from Beijing Institute of Technology in July 2020, advised by Prof. Meiling Wang.

Previously I was at Berkeley PATH, where I worked on Reinforcement Learning based ADAS (Advanced Driver-Assistance Systems) advised by Prof. Ching-Yao Chan. I also have experience at PKU POSS, where I did research about traversability analysis of autonomous vehicles using deep inverse reinforcement learning, advised by Prof. Huijing Zhao.
